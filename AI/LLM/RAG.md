# What is `RAG`
1. RAG `(Retrieval-Augmented Generation)`

2. Jab aap GPT yeah kisi bhe AI se direct Chat karte hai, to wo apni general knowledge (jo usne internet se seekhi hai) se jawab deta hai. Lekin jab aap chahte hain ke AI sirf aur sirf aapki PDF ko parhay aur bahar ki koi baat na kare, to uske liye RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) hi use hota hai, magar ek khaas setup ke saath jise "Grounded AI" kehte hain.
